French Telex

The keyboard package installed successfully.

Choose French Telex from the Keyman keyboard menu, then type French accents with Telex-like numeric suffixes.

Common shortcuts expand after a double space, for example cest -> c'est, yat -> y-a-t'il, and capitalized triggers such as Ya -> Il y a.

Double quotes become French guillemets, for example "bonjour" -> « bonjour ».
